## Brand & Style
The brand personality is authoritative yet welcoming, channeling the quiet confidence of a high-end heritage establishment. The target audience seeks a curated, unhurried experience that feels grounded in tradition and exclusivity. 

The design style is **Stately Minimalist with Tactile Accents**. It utilizes generous whitespace to create a sense of calm and "room to breathe," punctuated by intricate ornamental motifs. This system moves away from typical fast-paced e-commerce patterns in favor of a digital "concierge" feel, where every interaction is deliberate and refined. Key visual markers include delicate brass dividers, paisley-inspired micro-motifs, and a sophisticated layering of warm, creamy surfaces.

## Colors
The palette is anchored in a historical "Heritage Cream" background, which provides a warmer, more sophisticated alternative to pure white. 

- **Primary (Heritage Emerald):** Used for navigation bars, primary calls-to-action, and signature "cartouche" frames.
- **Secondary (Heritage Maroon):** Reserved for subtle alerts, high-end price tags, or critical accents to provide contrast without breaking the stately mood.
- **Accent (Heritage Brass):** Applied to rule dividers, icon details, and decorative paisley diamonds to evoke metal-work craftsmanship.
- **Surface (Heritage Ivory):** A lighter tint used for cards and interactive containers to create a soft, elevated lift from the background.
- **Typography:** "Ink" is used for primary body copy and headlines, while "Ink Soft" is utilized for secondary metadata and helper text to maintain a legible but gentle contrast.

## Typography
The typography system relies on the interplay between the authoritative, literary weight of **EB Garamond** (serving as a substitute for Cormorant) and the functional precision of **Inter**.

- **Display & Headlines:** Use EB Garamond with 500-600 weight. Headlines should feel editorial and spacious.
- **Section Labels:** These should be rendered in small-caps, italic EB Garamond with generous letter spacing to act as elegant "signposts" between sections.
- **Body Text:** Inter is utilized for high legibility in descriptions and product details. Tighten letter spacing slightly to maintain a cohesive, "printed" appearance.
- **Numerals:** Currency values must use tabular numerals for alignment in lists. The currency symbol (₹) is always rendered in EB Garamond Italic to contrast against the Inter numerals.

## Layout & Spacing
The design system employs a **Fixed Grid** philosophy on desktop to ensure content remains centered and prestigious, reminiscent of a printed menu. 

- **Whitespace:** Use "unhurried" spacing. Gaps between sections should be significant (80px+) to prevent the interface from feeling cluttered.
- **Grid:** A 12-column system is used for desktop with a maximum container width of 1140px.
- **Dividers:** Instead of simple lines, use "Heritage Brass" rule dividers. These consist of a 1px horizontal line punctuated in the center by a small paisley diamond motif.
- **Mobile Adaption:** On mobile, margins reduce to 20px, and the section gap compresses to 48px, but the commitment to whitespace remains to ensure the "Premium" feel is not lost.

## Elevation & Depth
Hierarchy is achieved through **Tonal Layering** and micro-textures rather than aggressive shadows.

- **Surface Tiering:** The background is #f6efe1. Elevated elements like product cards or modals use #fffaf0.
- **Depth:** Use very subtle, long-range ambient shadows (40px blur, 4% opacity #241910) to give the impression of heavy cardstock resting on a table.
- **Texturing:** Apply a damask micro-texture (6% opacity) to the primary background. This should be a subtle repeating pattern that is felt more than seen.
- **Frames:** Use "Heritage Emerald" for structural chrome (headers, footers) to box the content and provide a sense of stability.

## Shapes
The shape language is **Sharp and Architectural**. 

- **Corners:** Use 0px roundedness for all primary containers, buttons, and input fields to maintain a formal, colonial aesthetic. 
- **Cartouche Frames:** For featured items, use a "Cartouche" frame—this is a sharp rectangular border with Heritage Brass ornamental paisley accents at the four corners.
- **Image Treatment:** Product photography should be framed in simple, sharp-edged containers or within an arched "Emerald" frame for hero sections.

## Components
- **Buttons:** Primary buttons are solid "Heritage Emerald" with sharp corners and "Heritage Brass" text or "Heritage Ivory" text. Secondary buttons are "Heritage Brass" outlines with a 1px weight.
- **Cards:** Product cards use the "Heritage Ivory" surface. They are bordered by a 1px "Heritage Brass" frame. Title text is centered EB Garamond.
- **Input Fields:** Use "Ink Deep" for bottom-only borders (1px). Labels should be small-caps EB Garamond floating above the line.
- **Chips/Badges:** Use a "Heritage Maroon" background with Ivory text for "Exclusive" or "Limited" tags, styled as small, sharp-edged rectangles.
- **Lists:** Menu lists should use the "Brass Rule Divider" between items. Prices are aligned to the right using tabular numerals.
- **Cartouche Frame:** A signature component used for highlighting the "Chef's Special" or "Premium Selection," featuring a double-line border in Brass with paisley corner ornaments.
